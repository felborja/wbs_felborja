Our Purpose

At Zelting, we don't only embody creativity and innovation applied to the outdoor experience. We also support support initiatives with a positive impact on communities and the environment. We encourage people to think critically about what to take on their outdoor adventures.

We stand for respect and promote enjoying the essentials

At Zelting we want to be part of the solution to environmental and social problems by basing 100% of our products on at least one of five sustainables pillars:

FairTrade

We support ecosystem and wildlife conservation, not only in Europe, Patagonia and Asia.

With Cause

Our cooks only use local, fresh, and organic products to prepare your meals.

Recycled

All our partners only use reusable containers to package all your meals.

Sustainable Fibres

Going on vacation? Just pause your subscription, and we refund unused days.

Organic Cotton
